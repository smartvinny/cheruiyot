Course Description
Banchelor of Business Adminstration(BRA) Our BBA program equips students with essential business knowledge and skills. It covers areas such as marketing, finance, management, and entrepreneurship, preparing graduates for leadership roles in various industries.
Bachelor of Computer Science (BSc CS) In our BSc CS program, students delve into the world of computing, software development, and information technology. They gain expertise in programming, database management, and cybersecurity, paving the way for careers in the tech industry..
Bachelor of Arts in Communication and Media Studies Our Communication and Media Studies program explores the dynamics of communication in the digital age. Students study journalism, public relations, digital media, and communication theories, preparing them for roles in media, advertising, and public relations.
Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) in Early Childhood Education The B.Ed in Early Childhood Education program focuses on nurturing future educators who specialize in early childhood development. Students learn about child psychology, curriculum planning, and effective teaching methods for young learners.
